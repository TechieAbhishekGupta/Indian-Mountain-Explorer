Plan Your Trip

Accommodations

Find the perfect place to rest after a day of exploring the majestic Himalayan mountain parks. Our website offers information on a variety of accommodations near popular destinations in Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand, including:

  • Hotels: From luxurious resorts to budget-friendly hotels, find the ideal place to relax and recharge after your mountain adventures.
  • Homestays: Experience the warmth of local hospitality and comfort in cozy homestays, offering a genuine cultural experience.
  • Campgrounds: Get closer to nature by camping under the stars. Find campgrounds equipped with all necessary facilities in scenic locations.
  • Cottages & Lodges: Enjoy the rustic charm of a cottage or lodge nestled in the tranquil hills, offering comfort and peace.

Amenities

Make your trip even more enjoyable with our list of local amenities in the mountain regions, including:

  • Restaurants & Cafes: Explore local eateries and cafes offering delicious meals, traditional dishes, and snacks to fuel your adventure.
  • Shops: Need outdoor gear or supplies for your trekking trip? Visit local shops for everything from camping gear to fresh produce.
  • Tourist Information Centers: Get maps, local insights, and expert advice at tourist centers to help plan your activities and routes.
  • Transportation: Find transportation options like local buses, taxis, and bike rentals for easy access to trekking routes and nearby towns.

Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Before you embark on your Himalayan adventure, make sure to follow these important safety guidelines:

  • Trail Safety: Learn essential trail etiquette, what to do if you encounter wildlife, and how to minimize your environmental impact.
  • Weather & Conditions: Check the current weather conditions in the mountains, and know how to stay safe during varying weather patterns.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Familiarize yourself with basic first aid procedures, emergency contact numbers, and the nearest medical facilities.
