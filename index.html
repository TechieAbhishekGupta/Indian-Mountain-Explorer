Trekking & Hiking Trails

Explore the stunning trekking routes in regions like Kullu Valley, Spiti Valley, and Valley of Flowers. From moderate hikes to challenging alpine treks, there’s something for every adventure seeker.

Local Ski Areas

Hit the slopes in famous ski resorts like Auli (Uttarakhand) and Manali (Himachal Pradesh). Enjoy skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports in some of the most scenic alpine locations.

Free Activities

Immerse yourself in the great outdoors with a selection of free activities such as scenic drives through Tirthan Valley, wildlife spotting in Rajaji National Park, and hiking to iconic destinations like Triund and Roopkund.

Paid Activities

For those looking for an extra thrill, check out paid activities like river rafting in Rishikesh, camping in Great Himalayan National Park, or a peaceful experience at natural hot springs like those in Manikaran.
