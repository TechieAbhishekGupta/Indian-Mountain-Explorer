Activities in the Himalayan Mountain Parks

Explore a range of exciting activities in the stunning mountain regions of Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand.

Mountain Trekking & Skiing

Experience the thrill of trekking and skiing in the majestic Himalayas. Here are some popular activities:

  • Auli Ski Resort (Uttarakhand): Enjoy world-class skiing and snowboarding with breathtaking views of the Nanda Devi and Trishul peaks.
  • Solang Valley (Himachal Pradesh): Discover skiing, paragliding, and other winter sports in the stunning Solang Valley, near Manali.
  • Rohtang Pass (Himachal Pradesh): A popular destination for adventure lovers offering snow sports like skiing, snowboarding, and snow tubing.

Free Activities

Explore the beauty of the Himalayan regions with these free activities:

  • Hiking: Choose from a variety of trekking trails like the Valley of Flowers Trek and Hampta Pass Trek suitable for all skill levels.
  • Mountain Climbing: Test your limits with exhilarating mountain climbing experiences in regions like Spiti Valley and Gangotri.
  • Scenic Drives: Enjoy the stunning landscapes of the Kullu Valley and Spiti on picturesque drives.
  • Parks & Picnic Areas: Relax and unwind at beautiful parks and picnic spots such as Great Himalayan National Park and Rajaji National Park.
  • Nature Walks: Take leisurely walks in peaceful settings, such as Tirthan Valley or Rishikesh, to connect with nature.
  • Wildlife Viewing: Spot local wildlife like snow leopards, Himalayan tahr, and musk deer in parks like Jim Corbett and Great Himalayan National Park.
  • Local Markets: Browse through vibrant local markets in towns like Manali, Nainital, and Rishikesh for souvenirs and local handicrafts.
  • Cultural Events: Immerse yourself in the rich culture by attending festivals like the Kullu Dussehra or Nanda Devi Mela.

Paid Activities

For those looking for extra excitement, explore these paid activities:

  • Hot Springs: Rejuvenate your body and mind at natural hot springs like Manikaran and Tattapani.
  • River Rafting: Enjoy thrilling rafting experiences on the Ganges in Rishikesh or on the Beas in Manali.
  • Paragliding: Soar high above the beautiful valleys of Bir-Billing or Solang Valley for an unforgettable adventure.
  • Guided Trekking Tours: Opt for guided tours in areas like Kedarkantha or Chopta to discover hidden trails and local wildlife.
  • Adventure Excursions: Experience adrenaline-pumping activities such as rock climbing, mountain biking, and zip-lining in regions like Rishikesh and Manali.
  • Workshops & Classes: Take part in workshops such as traditional Himalayan cooking, handicraft classes, or yoga sessions offered by local instructors.
  • Equipment Rentals: Rent trekking gear, camping equipment, or skis for your outdoor activities in Manali and Auli.
  • Attractions & Landmarks: Visit historical and religious landmarks such as Badrinath Temple, Hadimba Temple, and Triund Hill.
